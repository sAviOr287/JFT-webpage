Jean-François Ton

Senior Research Scientist · ByteDance SEED

I’m Jean-François Ton (Jef) from Luxembourg, a Senior Research Scientist at ByteDance SEED in London.

My main research interests are in preference data, reward models, and multi-agent systems. More recently I have become more interested in evolutionary algorithms, active knowledge acquisition, and open-endedness.

During my DPhil I worked on kernel methods, causal inference, and Bayesian/Conformal uncertainty.

I hold a BSc from Imperial College London and an MSc and DPhil from the University of Oxford, advised by Prof. Yee Whye Teh and Prof. Dino Sejdinovic.

Outside of work, I like playing table tennis.
