Jean-François Ton

Senior Research Scientist · ByteDance Research / SEED

I build alignment and evaluation tooling for large language models, focusing on post-training workflows such as preference data pipelines, reward models, and multi-agent orchestration that keeps assistants reliable at scale.

Earlier in my career I explored kernel methods, causal inference, and uncertainty quantification—both Bayesian and conformal—to understand how models reason, calibrate, and generalise.

I earned a BSc at Imperial College London, followed by an MSc and DPhil at the University of Oxford, supervised by Prof. Yee Whye Teh and Prof. Dino Sejdinovic.

London, United Kingdom
