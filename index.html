Current research focus

Instruction-tuned LLMs

I develop post-training pipelines that combine reinforcement learning, direct preference optimisation, and scalable human feedback to align foundation models with nuanced policies.

Reward modelling & safety evaluation

From synthetic preference datasets to real-time feedback loops, my work builds robust reward models that respect safety constraints and uncover failure modes before launch.

Multi-agent systems

I prototype collaborative agent swarms that coordinate reasoning, tool use, and exploration—while leveraging my earlier research on kernels and conformal risk bounds to quantify uncertainty.
